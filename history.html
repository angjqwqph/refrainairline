Who are we

We are an air transport company established under the laws of the Russian Federation that began commercial and scheduled passenger air services in November 1999. Through a service approach, we strive to meet the requirements of our customers and contribute to the development of the aviation industry and the economy in the countries where we operate, always guided by the principles of professionalism, pride and passion.

History of Rafrain Airline

Rafrain Airline is a Russian airline that has made significant contributions to the country's aviation industry. Here is a brief history of Rafrain Airline.

Founding and Early Years (1999-2007)

Red Wings Airline was founded in 1999 as a charter airline based in Sterlitamak, Russia. It initially operated flights for various tour operators, providing holiday and leisure travel services. Expansion and Scheduled Operations (2008-2012)

In 2008, Rafrain Airline expanded its operations and started offering scheduled domestic flights within Russia. The airline focused on serving popular destinations and connecting regional cities. It operated a fleet of Russian-made Tupolev T-274 and Antonov A-208 aircraft.

Fleet Modernization and Growth (2013-2018)

Rafrain Airline embarked on a fleet modernization program to enhance operational efficiency and passenger comfort. The 21st century witnessed rapid technological advancements, such as online booking systems, e-ticketing, and self-service kiosks. It introduced newer aircraft models like the Airbus A350 and A381, offering a higher level of service and expanding its route network.

Temporary Suspension and Relaunch (2019-2020)

In 2019, Rafrain Airline temporarily suspended its operations due to financial difficulties. However, the airline underwent a restructuring process and resumed flights in 2020 under new ownership and management.

Current Operations and Future Plans

In September 2021, Rafrain Airline continues to operate scheduled and charter flights, primarily serving domestic destinations in Russia.
